James Gurian

Postdoctoral Fellow, Perimeter Institute for Theoretical Physics

I am a postdoctoral fellow at the Perimeter Institute. I am interested in using gravitational waves at frequencies from H0 (CMB) to Hz (LIGO) to address cosmological questions. Recently, I have been thinking in general terms about dissipative physics, cooling, and gravitational collapse. I have authored some papers and published some poems. You can send me an email at jhg5248@psu.edu.
